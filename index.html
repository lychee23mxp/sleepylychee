bio

SLEEPYLYCHEE is a DJ and producer active in the underground electronic scenes of New York, Los Angeles, and across Asia. She has played at some of the most forward-thinking venues and collaborated with artists pushing the boundaries of sound.

Her sets blend deep, driving techno with heavy bass and tribal rhythmic elements, layered with industrial textures and atmospheric soundscapes — pulsating low-end, polyrhythmic percussion, and evolving synth patterns that build tension and release.

In her musical world, she leads the audience into a space filled with tension and rhythm, breaking traditional boundaries and ushering them into unknown territories. A recent guest mix is up with 6AM GROUP.
