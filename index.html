About

DEEP, DRIVING TECHNO.
HEAVY BASS.
UNKNOWN TERRITORIES.

SLEEPYLYCHEE is a DJ and producer active in New York, Los Angeles, and across Asia's underground electronic scenes. She has played at some of the most forward-thinking venues and collaborated with cutting-edge artists pushing the boundaries of sound.

She crafts dynamic DJ sets that blend deep, driving techno with heavy bass and tribal rhythmic elements, layered with industrial textures and atmospheric soundscapes — featuring pulsating low-end, polyrhythmic percussion, and evolving synth patterns that build tension and release.

In her unique musical world, she leads the audience into a space filled with tension and rhythm — breaking traditional music boundaries and ushering the audience into unknown territories. Intense electronic pulses, ever-changing sound textures, and unique soundscapes collectively construct a marvelous and profound musical experience.
